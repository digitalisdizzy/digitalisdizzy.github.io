General Guides

Please follow these general guides when contacting me.

  • No Hello: Don't say hello and wait for a response before saying your problem!
  • Don't Ask to Ask: Similar to No Hello, don't ask to ask a question, just ask it!
  • XY Problem: Don't ask how to do your solution, ask the problem you want to solve.

Quick Questions

If you want to ask a quick question or say something short (e.g. asking for help with something I've made or telling me about something) and have access to my DMs (I allow messages from friends and people with mutual servers), send me a message on Discord, my username is digitalisdizzy.

If you don't have access to my DMs, please follow Larger Tasks.

Larger Tasks

If you can't DM me or have a bigger task, project or question for me (e.g. a commission, collaboration, or anything that would take me longer than a few hours to do), please email me at digital@place1.dev. Make sure the subject line is relevant, all of the information needed is in the body, and your email can receive messages as well as send them. I won't accept any friend requests on any platform unless you email me first.
